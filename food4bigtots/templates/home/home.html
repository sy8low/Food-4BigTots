ABOUT US

Convinced that no child is born a picky eater, my mum (Lai Kuan) cooked simple, healthy, and delicious food for me (Sze Yoong). She set up Food-4Tots in 2008 to share her experience with other mums. If she could do it, anyone can!

Food-4BigTots is my tribute to the original Food-4Tots. As an aspiring CS student, I took up the challenge of transforming the static WordPress website into a dynamic Flask web app, and giving it a fresh new look. I hope that it will continue to bring back sweet memories from my childhood and help everyone make healthy food that also tastes really good.
