Lai Kuan started cooking for her son, Sze Yoong, when he was a weaning toddler. While he was not a picky eater, he had his pet peeves: eggplant, zucchini, beetroot, cauliflower, coconut, papaya, watermelon... She wanted to nourish him with all the nutrients he needed, without relying on the usual tactics of blending vegetables into porridges and indulging him with french fries.

Inspired by Nicola Graimes’ “Children’s Healthy & Fun Cookbook”, Lai Kuan crafted simple yet delicious recipes that allowed their ingredients to shine through. Even though she started off with zero cooking knowledge, she not only managed to convince her son to try them, but even to ask for more! This motivated her to set up the original Food-4Tots in 2008 to share her experience with other mums around the world. If she could do it, anyone can!

16 years later, Sze Yoong is now a happy and healthy young man. After his A Levels, he decided to venture into Computer Science and learn to cook all the delicious goodies that he grew up eating. He challenged himself to redesign Food-4Tots, by refreshing its layout and transforming the static WordPress website into a dynamic Flask web app. He hopes that the new app, Food-4BigTots, will continue to bring back sweet memories from his childhood and help everyone make healthy food that also tastes really good.
