My mum (Lai Kuan) started cooking for me (Sze Yoong) when I was a weaning toddler. While I was not a picky eater, I had my pet peeves: eggplant, zucchini, beetroot, cauliflower, coconut, papaya, watermelon... She wanted to nourish me with all the nutrients I needed, without relying on the usual tactics of blending vegetables into porridges and indulging me with french fries.

Inspired by Nicola Graimes’ “Children’s Healthy & Fun Cookbook”, my mum crafted simple yet delicious recipes that allowed their ingredients to shine through. Even though she started off with zero cooking knowledge, she not only managed to convince me to try them, but even to ask for more! This motivated her to set up the original Food-4Tots in 2008 to share her experience with other mums around the world. If she could do it, anyone can!

{{ after }} years later, I am now a happy and healthy young man. After my A Levels, I decided to venture into Computer Science and also learn to cook all the delicious goodies that I grew up eating. I challenged myself to redesign Food-4Tots, by refreshing its layout and transforming the static WordPress website into a dynamic Flask web app. If you're interested to learn more about how I did it, do check out my Youtube video and GitHub repository.

I hope that the new app, Food-4BigTots, will continue to bring back sweet memories from my childhood and help everyone make healthy food that also tastes really good.
